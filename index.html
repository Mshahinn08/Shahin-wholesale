The Process: Transparent & Simple

A refined workflow built for partnership, clarity, and confidence.

1

Select & Request

Browse our catalog or website, select your desired models, and contact our team with your quantities.

2

Confirm & Deposit

Once the order is finalized, a 60% advance deposit is required to initiate production and secure your items.

3

Production & Full Transparency

As we prepare your order, we will send you photos and updates at every production stage. You see exactly what you are getting, just like a true partner.

4

Final Payment & Shipping

When the order is fully packed and ready, we will share the final photos. Upon receiving the remaining 40% payment, your shipment is dispatched, and the tracking/shipping details are immediately shared with you.
